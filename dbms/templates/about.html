About us


This is a research paper portal web application. It allows users to upload, access and comment on research papers. We believe this application satisfies a real need in the technical community.


When a researcher accomplishes a significant goal, he or she puts his or her findings in the form of a research paper, and publishes it through a well-known organization such as IEEE or ACM. This paper then becomes the primary source of information for faculty and students who desire to learn more about the work the paper discusses.


However, lack of accessibility of these papers is a major issue. Although portals do exist such as IEEE Xplore and ACM Digital Library, there is no singular research portal that caters to papers published in any conference throughout the world. This project aims to satisfy that need by providing researchers with a one-stop destination where they can access and comment on others' research papers, as well as upload their own.


This website also allows peers to comment and rate the paper, to allow researchers to gauge reactions to their work. It also allows users to see the number of views a paper has received, allowing them to gauge the paper's popularity.
